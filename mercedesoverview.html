PRODUCTS

GLS 250D
(red)

Under the hood, the standard GLA can be had with one petrol and one diesel engine. The new 1.3-litre petrol is tuned to develop 161bhp and 250Nm of torque while the potent 2.0-litre diesel motor puts out 188bhp and 400Nm of torque. The latter is also available with an all-wheel-drive configuration.

GLS 200D
(Gray)

This Mercedes Benz GLA 200 CDI Sport variant is equipped with a powerful 2.1-litre engine that displaces 2143cc. This motor is capable of producing 134bhp peak power along with a maximum torque output of 300Nm. It is skillfully mated with a 7-speed automatic transmission gearbox with a dual clutch setup.

GLS 400D
(matic)

The key features of the Mercedes-Benz GLS 400d 4MATIC include all-wheel drive, a 6-cylinder in-line diesel engine, a 9-speed automatic transmission, a spacious and elegant interior, advanced safety features, and a top speed of 238 km/h.
